# Milestone v2.0: Triple-Filing (CPPA + CA AG + PDF)

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2026-05-03
**Phases:** 9–11
**Total Plans:** 9 plans (14 source files, +1,513 net LOC)

## Overview

v2.0 added CPPA as the primary filing channel alongside the existing CA AG fax, giving every $1.99 filing three government submission paths: a guided CPPA online form walkthrough, a downloadable CPPA paper complaint PDF, and an auto-faxed complaint to the CA Attorney General. The milestone restructured the CA AG PDF from legal-letter style to a plain form layout, introduced a natural-language complaint narrative generator, and redesigned the success page to surface all three channels with status indicators.

## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

| Decision | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| CPPA becomes primary filing channel (not CA AG fax) | CPPA is primary CCPA enforcer since July 2023 |
| ADA complaints excluded from CPPA channel | ADA is not a CCPA violation — CPPA has no jurisdiction |
| AG PDF restructured to form-style (not legal letter) | Real consumers don't cite §1798.100; looks automated to government |
| Complaint description ≤2000 chars, no statute citations | CPPA form character limit; naturalness requirement |
| UUID-only access for /cppa-guide and /cppa-pdf | Allows guest filers to access their forms without login wall |
| Identity-H font encoding → Info dict for test searchability | pdf-lib Identity-H encoding makes drawn text unsearchable via latin1 scan |
| pdf-lib `useObjectStreams: false` | Keeps Info dict uncompressed and grep-findable in tests |
