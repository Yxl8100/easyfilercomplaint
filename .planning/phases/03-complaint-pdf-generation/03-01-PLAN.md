**Font loading:**
```typescript
const regularBytes = readFileSync(join(process.cwd(), 'src/assets/fonts/LiberationSerif-Regular.ttf'))
const boldBytes = readFileSync(join(process.cwd(), 'src/assets/fonts/LiberationSerif-Bold.ttf'))
```

**PDF creation sequence:**
1. `const pdfDoc = await PDFDocument.create()`
2. `pdfDoc.registerFontkit(fontkit)`
3. `const font = await pdfDoc.embedFont(regularBytes)`
4. `const boldFont = await pdfDoc.embedFont(boldBytes)`
5. `const page = pdfDoc.addPage([612, 792])` — US Letter
6. Set margins: `margin = 72`, `fontSize = 11`, `lineHeight = 16`, `maxWidth = 612 - 2 * margin`

**The 13 letter sections** (all using y-cursor pattern from pdf-filler.ts):

1. **Document header**: `page.drawText('PRIVACY COMPLAINT', { font: boldFont, size: 14 })` centered at top
2. **Filing date line**: `Filed via EasyFilerComplaint · {date}` in size 9, gray (0.5, 0.5, 0.5)
3. **Horizontal rule**: `page.drawLine(...)` from left margin to right margin
4. **To block**: "To: Office of the Attorney General\nState of California\n1300 I Street\nSacramento, CA 95814" (hardcoded for ca_ag; use agency lookup later)
5. **From block**: "From: {firstName} {lastName}\n{address}\n{city}, {state} {zip}\n{email}"
6. **Date line**: "Date: {formatted date}"
7. **Re / Subject line**: "Re: Privacy Complaint Against {targetName}" in bold
8. **Opening salutation**: "Dear Attorney General,"
9. **Body paragraphs**: Call `generateComplaintText(filingData, 'ca_ag')` — convert Filing to FilingData shape first, then render with word-wrap loop
10. **Relief requested**: "I respectfully request that your office investigate this matter and take appropriate enforcement action to protect consumer privacy rights."
11. **Prior contact disclosure**: If `filing.priorContact === true`, add "I previously contacted {targetName} regarding this issue. {priorContactDetails}"
12. **Closing**: "Respectfully submitted,\n\n{firstName} {lastName}"
13. **Footer**: At bottom of last page: "EasyFilerComplaint · easyfilercomplaint.com · Filing ID: {filingReceiptId}" in size 8, gray
