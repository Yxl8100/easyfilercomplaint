## Typography

| Role | Size | Weight | Line Height | Font family | Usage |
|------|------|--------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| Body | 16px | 500 | 1.625 (relaxed) | Inter (`font-body`) | Paragraph text, FAQ answers, legal page body |
| Label | 11px (0.68rem) | 400 | 1.0 | JetBrains Mono (`font-mono`) | Section eyebrows, badge text, nav links, CTAs (`text-[11px] tracking-[0.1em] uppercase`) |
| Heading | 28px–30px | 700 | 1.25 (tight) | Fraunces (`font-serif`) | H2 section headings (`text-3xl`) |
| Display | 36px–48px | 700 | 1.2 (tight) | Fraunces (`font-serif`) | H1 hero heading (`text-4xl md:text-5xl`) |

Notes:
- Body weight is 500 per `globals.css` (`p { font-weight: 500 }`). Base body is 450 (`body { font-weight: 450 }`).
- All existing mono labels use 9px (`text-[9px]`) for eyebrow/micro-labels and 11px for interactive labels. Maintain this distinction.
- Legal page body text uses 16px at weight 500 and line-height 1.625.
- Legal page H1 uses `text-4xl font-bold font-serif` matching the pattern in `07-RESEARCH.md` Pattern 1 example.

Source: `src/app/page.tsx`, `src/app/globals.css`, `tailwind.config.ts`, `07-RESEARCH.md` Pattern 1.

### FAQ Questions and Answers (verbatim for MKTG-03)

1. **Q: "What happens after I file?"**
   A: "Your complaint is faxed directly to the California Attorney General's office. You receive a copy of your complaint PDF by email along with a unique filing receipt ID. Government agencies handle complaints on their own timelines."

2. **Q: "Is EasyFilerComplaint a law firm?"**
   A: "No. EasyFilerComplaint is a complaint filing service. We prepare and transmit your complaint document to the appropriate government office. We do not provide legal advice and no attorney-client relationship is created."

3. **Q: "What types of privacy complaints can I file?"**
   A: "Currently we support three types: privacy tracking violations (CCPA), website accessibility barriers, and video sharing privacy violations. All complaints are submitted to the California Attorney General."

4. **Q: "Will I get a refund if my complaint is rejected?"**
   A: "The $1.99 fee covers the filing service itself — preparing and transmitting your complaint. Agency decisions about whether to act on a complaint are outside our control. Fees are non-refundable after submission."

5. **Q: "How do I know my complaint was filed?"**
   A: "You receive a filing receipt email at the address you provided. The email includes your unique receipt ID (formatted EFC-YYYYMMDD-XXXXX), a copy of your complaint PDF, and confirmation that it was transmitted to the CA AG."
